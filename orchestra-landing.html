Core capabilities

Built for modern teams

From hackathon teams to remote startups — Orchestra adapts to how you work.

Intelligent SDLC Orchestration
The AI acts as a technical architect that maps out exact engineering dependencies, ensuring backend, frontend, and database tasks are sequenced correctly.
Smart Workload Distribution
Analyzes individual team member strengths based on past activity to recommend optimal workload distribution and eliminate bottlenecks.
Cross-Platform Integration
Quietly connects GitHub, Figma, Discord, and other workspaces to build a live, visual, interconnected brain of your team's knowledge.
Clover AI Assistant
Ask complex questions like "Who changed this file last night and why?" and get contextual answers with direct links and summaries.
100% Private & Secure
All data stays within your workspace. No third-party training on your code, designs, or conversations.
Zero Alignment Meetings
The development cycle flows seamlessly from architecture to deployment without constant status check-ins.
How it works

From idea to roadmap
in under a minute

01

Describe your app idea

Paste a description of what you want to build — no technical jargon required. Orchestra parses intent, not syntax.

02

AI generates your blueprint

Within seconds, Orchestra maps every engineering dependency — database schemas, API contracts, frontend components — in the right order.

03

Tasks auto-assign to your team

Orchestra analyzes each member's GitHub activity and skills to distribute work optimally. Connect your tools and let it run.

04

Progress tracks itself

As your team commits code and updates designs, Orchestra automatically moves tasks through the board — zero manual updates.
